## Description (both stores)
Four billion kilometers from home, your survey vessel AURORA hears a signal — clean, patterned, and coming from a place the charts say is empty. It isn't noise. It isn't a weapon. Something out there has been waiting a very long time, and it just noticed us.

You are **Deepwatch**, humanity's deep-space mission control. Across three acts you'll:
• **DECODE** the signal — pull meaning out of static, one prime number at a time.
• **COORDINATE** under pressure — keep your ship and crew safe when contact gets dangerous.
• **DEDUCE** the truth — and decide, word by word, what humanity says back.

**Play solo** as Flight Director with a full crew of characters at the consoles — or **link up with friends** by share-code and each take a station. You only win by talking to each other.

A story-rich, hopeful adventure about patience, listening, and what it means to answer someone in the dark. **No violence. No gore. No profanity.** Just the vastness — and a friend in it, if you're brave enough to say hello.

Five endings. One question from the dark: *Will you remember us?*
