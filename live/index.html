THE LAST SIGNAL

A free cooperative text adventure.

What is this?

A story-driven game played entirely in your browser, with no downloads or accounts.

How to play

1) Open multiple tabs. Each tab is a different research station.

2) Pick a station and enter a callsign.

3) Read the scene, then choose what to do.

4) Use the Inter-Station Channel to share clues with your other stations.

5) Check your objectives at the top.

6) The ending depends on cooperation. Share findings before the mission timer runs out.
